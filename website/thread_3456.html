1) Basics
-----------
In the game, Sonic will collect items after defeating enemies and bosses,
helping certain Townspeople, and opening treasure chests. Either way, Sonic
will always have items after completing missions. Once a mission is completed,
players will be taken to a screen where they can choose to identify the items
they’ve collected. Use Identification Points (IP), which are also added at the
end of missions, identify the items of your choice. Once an item has been
identified, you can’t take back the IP you spent and all items that weren’t
identified are discarded, so chose wisely.
Note: Sonic can only collect up to 12 items maximum.

Types
All items belong to individual groups based on their icons.

Weapons- their icon is a sword
Armor- their icon is a metal glove
Medicines- their icon is a round beaker
Necklaces- their icon is a pearl necklace
Books- their icon is a book cover
Flowers- their icon is a flower
Gem Stones- their icon is a Gem/Emerald
Minerals- their icon is an oval shaped rock
Rings- their icon is a ring
Toys- their icon is the Pawn Chess game piece
Sack/Food- their icon is a bag
Present Box- their icon is a box shaped present.
