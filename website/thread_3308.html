SBK的FACE TO FAITH發行後，隨及傳出這樣的事......

大家應該都知道演唱WITH ME的EMMA吧...
雖然這首歌很好聽，但.........

這是她以ALL ENDS的主唱身分演唱的最後一首歌

EMMA表示，他對於MUSIC以及TOURING方面感到很厭倦
於是他決定離開ALL ENDS
不過，其他ALL ENDS的團員尊重EMMA的決定

他們表示，就算分開了，他們還是好朋友

以下為EMMA留給粉絲們的留言

Fans ,friends and family!

For the last 6 years All Ends and its members has been everything to me. All the joy, happiness and love I have felt through the years from everyone in AE, from the fans and everyone who has ever worked with us, I will never forget! But now I feel that I have to leave my spot to someone who has the same love and engagement as the rest of the guys have for AE. I feel that I need to put my focus on my family and friends now, and I am sorry to say that there is no room for AE...

Thanks, everyone for all the support that you have given me through the years!
I will never, ever forget!!

/////Emma Gelotte
