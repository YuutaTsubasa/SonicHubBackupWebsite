關卡只有Model Sonic的Stage 6和Test Stage(選Stage 6外也會自動把Stage選作Test Stage)

Controls:
Mouse->Camera control
W->Forward(if 3D camera)/Look up(if 2D camera)
S->Backward(if 3D camera)/Crouch(if 2D camera and standing)/Roll(if 2D camera and moving)
A->Left
D->Right
Left mouse click->Jump/Spindash(if crouching)
Left mouse click+left mouse click->Homing attack(if target present)/Jumpdash(if no target present)/Firedash(if fire shield active)
Right mouse click->Crouch(if no boost stored and not moving)/Roll(if no boost stored and moving)/Boost(if boost is stored)/Grind fast(if grinding)
Left mouse click+middle mouse click->Stomp/Bounce(if bubble shield active)
Left mouse click+right mouse click->Boostdash(if boost is stored)
E->Light speed dash
Middle mouse click+W+(A or D)->Drift
Middle mouse click->Stop rolling(if rolling)/Stop sliding(if sliding)
Q->Slide
TAB->Go super state(if rings are 50 or more)
Left CTRL/Left SHIFT->Left quick step/Left rail jump(if on rail)
SPACE->Right quick step/Right rail jump(if on rail)
ESC->Pause
