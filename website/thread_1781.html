(以下DATA取自http://www.gamefaqs.com/console/wii/file/932811/47240)

PAGE 1
MISSION: LOST PROLOGUE 16 - PARAGRAPH 16
Splash Jump onto the top platform with a Platinum Ring. Brake for a few
seconds, and you’ll see something big and purple swimming through the air. It’s
Big the Cat!
------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 2
MISSION: SAND OASIS 1 - GO FOR THE GOAL
On the strip of land where pillars are rising out of the ground, brake for a
few seconds before you jump over the second falling pillar. You’ll see Big
waving at you behind a bush thing.
------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 3
MISSION: SAND OASIS 4 - NO PEARLS
When the four pillars start to fall, brake for a few seconds in front of the
first one on the right (It has a genie on it.). You’ll see Big swimming around
with a Frog.
------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 4
MISSION: DINOSAUR JUNGLE 6 - STEALTH ATTACK
After running down the dinosaur, brake for a few seconds on the grassy area as
you get near the flower spirit. You’ll see Big holding his head.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 5
MISSION: DINOSAUR JUNGLE 9 - SPECIAL CHALLENGE
When you get to the river, ride the log into the flying pot. Fly as high as
you can, jump and do a homing-attack into the spring. You’ll jump automatically
for a bit and then land on a grassy, circular platform with a genie egg. Brake
for a few seconds, and you’ll see Big waving at you under a dinosaur.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 6
MISSION: EVIL FOUNDRY 2 - DIEHARD CHALLENGE
As you start the level, do a homing-attack into the tank the Golem’s holding,
then attack it’s head, and then attack the magic carpet. Brake for a few
seconds after getting onto the pathway, and you’ll see Big on a torch lamp on
the ceiling.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 7
MISSION: EVIL FOUNDRY 8 - PERFECT CHALLENGE
When you see the Golem, jump onto the cylinder and brake for a few seconds.
You’ll see big sitting on the edge of a pit of lava.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 8
MISSION: LEVITATED RUIN 5 - SPECIAL CHALLENGE
As you approach the lever to switch the second mirror, brake for a few
seconds. You’ll see big balancing on top of a tower.
----------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 9
MISSION: LEVITATED RUIN 10 - BEAT THE CLOCK
As you get near the series of gargoyle’s, brake for a few seconds. You’ll see
Big behind an empty pedestal.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 10
MISSION: PIRATE STORM 1 - GO FOR THE GOAL
When you reach the catapult after flying on a cannon rocket thing, raise the
catapult halfway and flick the Wii Remote to (hopefully) land in the crow’s
nest. Brake for a few seconds and you’ll see Big sitting on a mast farther away.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 11
MISSION: PIRATE STORM 5 - RAMPAGE!
When you come to an area with two very tall platforms, attack the
disappearing-reappearing genie on the first tall platform, and then land and
brake on it for a few seconds. You’ll see Big far away, looking at some type or
fort or something.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 12
MISSION: SKELETON DOME 5 - RAMPAGE!
When you enter the air launcher that fires you into a double-file line of
pearls, wait in it for a few seconds. You’ll see Big sitting in the middle of
the arena.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 13
MISSION: SKELETON DOME 8 - NO PEARLS
When you land on the group of rock platforms before the swinging axe things,
brake for a few seconds. You’ll see Big sitting on a giant skeleton in the
area, waving at you.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 14
MISSION: NIGHT PALACE 9 - DIEHARD CHALLENGE
Don’t bother with the turbo start here. Back up as soon as you start the level
to the staircase, and brake for a few seconds. You’ll see Big lying down on the
ground.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 15
MISSION: NIGHT PALACE 11 - BEAT THE CLOCK
In the room that turns upside-down, don’t grind along the rail. Drop onto what
used to be the ceiling (The floor.), past the first line on the ground, spike
ball, and stand next to the first pillar thing on the second line. Brake there
for a few seconds. You’ll see Big between two pillars.

SORRY忘了給

PAGE 1
LOST PROLOGUE 16: PRAGRAPH 16
用SPLASH JUMP到上方有個Platinum Ring的平台上
煞車幾秒，你將看到一個紫色東東再空中游來游去(BIG THE CAT)

PAGE 2
SAND OASIS 1: GO FOR THE GOAL
到有柱子從地面浮出的地帶
在跳過第二個倒下的柱子前煞車
你將看到BIG在某物後面對你揮手

PAGE 3
SAND OASIS 4: NO PEARLS
一樣也是到有柱子倒下的地帶
在右邊第一個柱子前煞車(有GENIE在上方)
你將看到BIG與一隻青蛙游來游去

PAGE 4
DINOSAUR JUNGLE 6:STEALTH ATTACK
當你跑下DINOSAUR到地面時，在FLOWER GENIE前煞車
你將看到BIG抱著他的頭不曉得在做啥

PAGE 5
DINOSAUR JUNGLE 9: SPECIAL CHALLENGE
第一次進到FLYING POT時，設法飛高一點
高到可以用HOMING ATTACK打到最上方的SPRING(就是要打)
SONIC會自己跳，最後會到一個有一顆蛋的圓形平台
停在蛋附近(煞車)，你會看到BIG在恐龍下對你揮手

PAGE 6
EVIL FOUNDRY 2: DIEHARD CHALLENGE
剛起跑時，打機器人手上拿的桶子，再打他的頭
再打魔毯，DASH到上面(最好在籠子附近)煞車
你會看到BIG坐在天花板上的火燈

PAGE 7
EVIL FOUNDRY 8: PERFECT CHALLENGE
當你看到機器人(GOLEM)時，跳到它前面的小柱子上
煞車，你將看到BIG坐在岩漿坑邊(SONIC V.S. IFRIT GOLEM之處)

PAGE 8
LEVITATED RUIN 5: SPECIAL CHALLENGE
接近第二個開關時(注意: 一旦SONIC跳到開關前做READY POSE就來不及了)煞車
你會發現BIG在塔的最上方搞平衡(XD?)

PAGE 9
LEVITATED RUIN 10: BEAT THE CLOCK
當你接近石像區時煞車，你會發現BIG在一個無石像的座後

PAGE 10
PIRATE STORM 1: GO FOR THE GOAL
過了「火箭炮(???)空中TRAVELING」到了第一個投「球(SONIC)」機
喬成45度角然後FIRE!!!
(需要一點運氣)你將落在烏鴉巢上(不知是不是)
煞車，你將看到BIG在遠處的船祪上

PAGE 11
PIRATE STORM 5: RAMPAGE!
當你到了兩個超~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~高(也沒那麼高啦(誤))的平台區
攻擊那個猛出現又消失的GENIE，之後落地，煞車
你會發現BIG不曉得又再找啥東東了

PAGE 12
SKELETON DOME 5: RAMPAGE!
當你進到會把你FIRE到兩行PEARL的AIR LAUNCHER
脈衝太緊(別衝太快)先停在內抵(裡面)在說
你會看到BIG坐在競技場中央

PAGE 13
SKELETON DOME 8: NO PEARLS
當你落再斧頭前的一群平台上
煞車，你將發現BIG坐在巨大的骨骼上向你揮手

PAGE 14
NIGHT PALACE 9: DIEHARD CHALLENGE
剛起跑時，注意!!!不要用任何DASH SKILL
你就退退退退退退退...........................................到不能再退
煞車，你會發現BIG在地上打轉(開始思念家鄉了吧，BIG)

PAGE 15
NIGHT PALACE 11: BEAT THE CLOCK
到了上下顛倒的ROOM，不要上軌道
落在天花板(地板)上
閃掉第一航柱子和刺球
停再第二行第一個的SIDE
你會發現BIG在兩個柱子間(BIG:好思念FROGGY，FROGGY，等等我!!!)

P.S. 以上我只解除1~7和9和14
另外，若有錯跟我說
