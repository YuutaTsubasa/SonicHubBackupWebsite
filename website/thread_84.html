2. Sand Oasis
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
2.a. Sand Oasis 1: Go for the Goal
Find King Shahryar!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Ring Skill Needed: Splash Jump(Lv. 42)
After a section on paths over the water, you will find a Spiky
Ball floating over a switch. Carefully press the switch and two
high pillars rise up. You need to use Splash Jump to get over them,
then use a quick Jump Cancel to land on top. A line of enemies
will appear up there, Homing Attack to the Fire Soul.

FS #2:
Inside the fiery building, clearly seen floating in the air above
flames surrounded by pearls.

FS #3:
After Shahra says \"Just past that pier is the town\", there is a
bridge with two brown paths. If you look between the paths, there
is a lower rail below. Jump down and land on that rail and an
invisible spring at the end of it will send you into a Fire Soul.


~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
2.b. Sand Oasis 2: Diehard Challenge
Don\t get Defeated!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Defeat the very first enemy in the mission

4. Evil Foundry
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
4.a. Evil Foundry 1: Go for the Goal
Head for the Center!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Helpful Ring Skill: Fire Lock-on
First enemy in the mission is a giant robot. Jump onto the barrels
in front of it. Then wait until the enemy is closest to you, then
jump and Homing Attack it. When you bounce off the enemy, you will
see the Fire Soul above it surrounded by pearls. It\s a lot easier
to grab with Fire Lock-on skill.

FS #2:
After the first hallway with fans and stuff

5. Levitated Ruin
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
5.a. Levitated Ruin 1: Go for the Goal
Find Sinbad!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
In the area where Shahra says \"Watch out for those spikes, Sonic!\"
are several rails. Try to get on the left-most set of Rails, you
will have to charge a jump and Homing Attack an enemy at one point,
to reach a higher area with a switch. Hit the switch to make a
spring appear that will send you into a Fire Soul.

FS #2:
Ring Skill Needed: Soul Gauge
When you land after getting Fire Soul 1 or just simply going
through the level the normal way, you will be trapped in a room
with a gargoyle. Defeat the gargoyle first, then use Time Break in
the room, you will see a Fire Soul appear on the pedestal the
gargoyle was sitting on.

FS #3:
After the first time chasing the gargoyle through the air, you
will come to one set of rails, then some enemies in front of a
magic stone, IGNORE THEM! Go past here to the next set of rails,
the Fire Soul is on a rail to the far left.


~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
5.b. Levitated Ruin 2: Rampage!
Defeat 1 Enemy!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Behind where you start, so just backtrack.

FS #2:
In the air, you will probably hit it as you swing the rope.

FS #3:
Helpful Ring Skill: Fire Lock-on(EF5 Gold Medal)
Near the 1 enemy in this mission after you jump off the rope at
full swing. I would highly recommend Fire Lock-on, I got it
without it somehow, but it\s really hard to!


~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
5.c. Levitated Ruin 5: Special Challenge
Open Sinbad\s Cage!
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Helpful Ring Skill: Fire Lock-on(EF 5 Gold)
Clearly seen below a rope in the second tower. I would recommend
jumping at the rope, but waiting for Fire Lock-on to home in on
the Fire Soul instead of the rope.

FS #2:
In the third tower, there is a switch between two spiky balls.
Jump over the first ball then use a Jump Cancel to land on the
switch. A spring will appear and send you into a series of Air
Launchers facing straight upwards, make sure to especially launch
the third one as far as possible to get a Fire Soul.

FS #3:
It\s on the side of the wall outside the fourth tower. Use the
catapult

8.a. [/font][font=Tahoma]Night[/font]
[font=Tahoma]Palace[/font][font=Tahoma][size=11pt] 1: Go for the Goal

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
FS #1:
Magic Carpet section, clearly seen between two spiky balls. Very

FS #2:
Required Ring Skills: Slowed Search or Sub S-Search (45 Silver
Medals or Lv. 44), Soul Gauge
Helpful Ring Skill: Fire Lock-on.
High above the exit of the third poison gas room. This one is very
tricky, you might have to let the gas kill you, then gather more
Pearls to re-try. Now run to the back of the room, then before the
middle pillar at the back of the room reconstitutes itself, use
Time Break and then use Slowed Search to Homing Attack the top of
the pillar as it\s going up to eventually reach the Fire Soul.
